Foodies Unite

Are you a foodie constantly looking for new places to eat? Food trucks are a great way to explore different cuisines around you. Our app focuses on helping users find food trucks that they want to eat at easily. For operators, our app is a great marketing tool and a way to organize your collection of trucks. We allow foodies and vendors to find one another quickly!

Location

Simply input your location using the app to locate trucks within a specific radius.

Organization

Filter by cuisine, rating, or price to select your truck of choice! Owners can also organize their fleet and add/remove pictures and menu items easily.

Convenience

Diners can quickly locate a truck and view their location and time of departure. Never have to guess what's to eat again!
